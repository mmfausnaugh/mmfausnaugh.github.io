Research

I am an observatonal astronomer. I mostly work on methods and data analysis in the time domain.

My main expertise is in extragalactic astrophysics, particularly Active Galactic Nuclei and Supernovae.

I also support the TESS mission at MIT and NASA Ames. I am the Leader of the TESS Data Analysis Working Group (DAWG), which approves TESS data for release to the public archive.
